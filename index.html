Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

What videos can I watch on StreamTogether? +
You can watch any video using a direct video URL (like an .mp4 link). We also have a library of public domain and Creative Commons licensed movies you can browse for free.
Do my friends need to create an account? +
Nope! The host creates a room, and friends can join just by clicking the shared link. No sign-up needed to be a guest in someone else's party.
How does the video sync work? +
When anyone presses play, pause, or seeks to a different timestamp, that event is instantly broadcast to all viewers in the room via Supabase Realtime. Everyone's player jumps to the same position within milliseconds.
How many people can join a watch party? +
Currently up to 20 people can join a single room. We're working on increasing this limit. The host controls playback for everyone.
Is StreamTogether free to use? +
Yes — completely free. Create unlimited rooms, invite unlimited friends, and chat away. No credit card required.
